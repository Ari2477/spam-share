User Guide

Learn how to use Facebook Share Master effectively and safely

1

Get Your Facebook Cookie

Log into Facebook in your browser, open Developer Tools (F12), go to Application/Storage tab, find Cookies section, and copy the entire "facebook.com" cookie value.

Important: Never share your cookie with anyone!

2

Add Your Account

Go to "Add Account" page, enter a name for your account and paste your Facebook cookie. Your cookie is stored locally and only visible to you.

Security: Each user can only see and edit their own accounts.

3

Configure Share Settings

In the Share Tool page, select your account, enter the Facebook post link you want to share, set the share count (max 50), and adjust the share delay (default: 1ms).

Tip: Use 1ms delay for maximum speed, or increase for more safety.

4

Start Sharing

Click "Start Sharing" to begin the process. Monitor real-time progress in the Process History page and see detailed statistics in the Statistics page.

Real-time: All processes are logged in real-time for monitoring.

5

Monitor & Analyze

Check the Dashboard for overview and Statistics page for detailed analytics. Use the real-time logs to monitor all activities.

Safety: Built-in limits prevent excessive sharing that could trigger Facebook's security systems.
