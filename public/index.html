Instructions

1 How to Get Facebook Cookie

  1. Log in to Facebook in your browser
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Go to the "Application" tab (Chrome) or "Storage" tab (Firefox)
  4. Click on "Cookies" and select "https://www.facebook.com"
  5. Copy the entire cookie string

2 How to Use Delay Setting

  1. 500ms - Fast but higher risk of detection
  2. 1000ms - Balanced speed and safety (recommended)
  3. 2000ms+ - Slow but safest option
  4. Delay adds time between each share operation
  5. Total time = (Number of shares × Delay) + processing time

3 Best Practices

  • Start with small batches (5-10 shares)
  • Use delays of at least 1000ms between shares
  • Test with one share first to verify cookie works
  • Use different cookies for large sharing operations
  • Monitor your Facebook account for any warnings
